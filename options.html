Full-page screenshots for Firefox

🦊 Welcome to FoxSS!

FoxSS captures a screenshot of an entire web page — not just the part you can see. It scrolls the page (or an inner scrolling panel) for you, stitches every screen together, and saves the result.

  • One click, whole page. Click the fox in your toolbar, or press Alt+Shift+P.
  • Long pages, sorted. Tall pages are split into screen-sized images and can be bundled into a single .zip.
  • Yours only. Everything happens on your device — no accounts, no tracking, nothing sent anywhere.
